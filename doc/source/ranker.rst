Ranker
==========

Ranker sorts the objects in the Corpus by a given scoring function and annotates the objects with their rankings.

Example usage: `Ranking users in r/Cornell by the number of comments they have made <https://github.com/CornellNLP/Cornell-Conversational-Analysis-Toolkit/blob/master/convokit/ranker/demos/ranker_demo.ipynb>`_.
