Coordination
============

Computes Coordination features as described in this `paper <https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~cristian/Echoes_of_power.html>`_.

Example usage: `exploring the balance of power in the U.S. Supreme Court <https://github.com/CornellNLP/Cornell-Conversational-Analysis-Toolkit/blob/master/examples/coordination/examples.ipynb>`_.
